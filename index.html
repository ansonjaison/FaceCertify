⚠️ The Old Way

Slow Manual Verification: Traditional ID checks are time-consuming and prone to human error, causing delays.
High Impersonation Risk: Fake IDs and collusion make it difficult to prevent fraudulent exam-takers.
Inefficient Paperwork: Manual attendance tracking is cumbersome and leads to administrative burdens and errors.

✨ The FaceCertify Way

Real-Time Biometric ID: Verifies a student's identity in seconds using their unique facial features.
Automated & Secure: By automating the process, we eliminate human error and secure the exam environment.
Dynamic Seating: Once verified, students are instantly shown their classroom and seat number on a visual map.

Core System Features

🛡️

Ironclad Security

Leverages a powerful SVM model to prevent impersonation and ensure only authorized students enter the exam hall.

⏱️

Automated & Instant

Eliminates manual paperwork by automatically logging attendance and displaying seat allocations in real-time.

⚙️

Effortless Administration

A centralized dashboard gives administrators complete control to manage students, models, and monitor live activity.

A Simple 3-Step Process

01

Capture

Student approaches the portal and their image is captured via a standard webcam.

02

Verify

Our AI model instantly matches the face against the secure student database with a 70% confidence threshold.

03

Access

Verified students see their seat number, and their attendance is logged automatically in a CSV file.
